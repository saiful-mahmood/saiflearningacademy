HEY THERE! WELCOME TO MY SITE

Learn with confidence!

I graduated from Boston College's Carroll School of Management in 2017 with a major in Finance and Information Systems. Throughout my college career, I have helped many middle and high school students stay on top of their academics in order to get into their dream college and careers. I found that through my private and group tutoring sessions, my students truly began to enjoy what they were learning and were motivated to be successful!

I take the time to understand how my students learn best and try to give them the tools necessary to be lifelong learners and self-starters. My goal is for each student that I tutor to learn with confidence! I really take the time to understand the aspects of their education that are hindering their progress, and then we work together to tackle those challenges one at a time.

Modes of delivering instruction are too slow to change with our advances in technology. It's time we consider shifting our mindset and adapt to the ways of teaching that are more effective. With the chaotic nature of changes that have happened in our world due to Covid, I witnessed many bright students fall behind in their schooling. My goal to simply be available for support when they need my help the most.

Beyond the tutoring, I am always ready to offer career advice and resources to help my students succeed in their academic career and beyond!

Services offered

Contact Saif to schedule a free consultation. The purpose of the first meeting is to understand expectations for both student and teacher. Tutoring can be carried out in person or virtually and must be agreed upon in the initial consult. Below are some examples of the services that I offer.

Remote Teaching

Group or individual Zoom sessions are hosted for ease of tutoring remotely.

In Person

Tutor meets student at home or class to carry out tutoring.

Asynchronus Learning

Students can watch recorded videos for reviewing content that was covered in previous sessions.

Essay Revisions

Writing workshops and essay revisions throughout the semesters.

SAT Prep

SAT math, reading and writing prep.

Mentorship

College counseling and career advice.
